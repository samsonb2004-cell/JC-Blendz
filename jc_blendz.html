Our Story

The Art of
The Perfect Cut

JC Blendz is the atelier of Jacopo Sublet — a barber shaped by three cultures and a relentless pursuit of precision. Born with Italian sensibility, refined through French aesthetics, and now rooted in the elegant rhythm of Viennese life.

Every cut is a study in architecture. Every fade, a gradient of intention. Whether you arrive for a sharp business line-up or a textured European crop, you leave with something that fits not just your hair — but who you are.

At JC Blendz, the chair is a sanctuary. No rush. No compromise. Just craft.
